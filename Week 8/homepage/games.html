Play Video Games

I play video games since I can remember, I grew up playing with a play station 2 and online browser games like club penguin, I don't think I have lived the generation of classic games, but I don't mind having squeezed the most out of the seventh and eighth generation of consoles.

My favourite game



Outer Wilds


Outer wilds is a game about exploring space, there is no better definition, what makes it my favorite game is that the only limiting factor in the game is knowledge, everything can be done from the first moment, but what stops the player is not knowing, it motivates you to keep exploring and manages to be sentimental without even having a linear story, but I think that I have no words to describe how perfect this game is, the platinum of this game is the best platinum than I can ever get

And it has a good soundtrack too
